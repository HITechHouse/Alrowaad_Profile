Who We Are

Al-Rowaad General Trading specializes in importing and distributing premium foodstuff in the Sultanate of Oman. We are dedicated to providing outstanding food products imported primarily from Syria, Turkey, and Egypt, prioritizing high quality and meeting the diverse needs of the Omani market.

With massive expertise in the food distribution sector, we operate through a highly organized network that ensures our products are delivered to customers swiftly and efficiently across all regions of the Sultanate.

Our Subsidiary

Al-Rowaad operates its extensive market reach through our specialized distribution arm: Durrat Al-Sham Distribution Company. They handle direct distribution to retail stores, hypermarkets, and wholesalers across all Omani governorates.
